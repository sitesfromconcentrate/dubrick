Philosophy

A family-run business for generations, our team brings over 35 years of industry experience. We manage your property as if we lived next door, supported by a fully computerized accounting environment.

Tenant Screening & Marketing

We aggressively market vacancies through our internal listing system, local newspapers, and online platforms. All potential tenants undergo rigorous screening, including:

  • Credit and Solvency Checks
  • Employment Verification
  • Previous Landlord References
  • Income History Analysis

Accounting & Financials

Our computerized systems provide transparency and ease. We handle mortgage payments directly from your account balance and provide two detailed monthly reports:

  • Transaction Summary: tenant names, rents paid, and arrears
  • General Ledger: summary of receipts and payments by expense category

Operations & Upkeep

We leverage bulk purchasing power to keep costs down while maintaining curb appeal. Our maintenance services include:

  • 24/7 Emergency Service for Tenants
  • Regular Preventative Inspections
  • Insurance Adjuster Consultations
  • Tendered Contracts for Large-Scale Projects
